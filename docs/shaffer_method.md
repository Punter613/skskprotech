# 🔧 The Shaffer Extraction Method
**Application:** Overriding Ford TSB 08-7-6 for F-150 / Triton 3V Fused Spark Plugs

## 🛠️ The Shop Procedure
* **The Prep:** Clear the plug wells entirely with compressed air before backing anything out.
* **The Initial Crack:** Give the stuck plug a fractional turn just to break the initial carbon seal.
* **The Liquid Splice:** Drop your dedicated penetration compound down the bore and let it sit to dissolve the carbon jacket.
* **The Extraction Pass:** Utilize a steady hand-torque back-and-forth rocking routine to clean the threads out manually without shearing the ground shield lower shell.

> 📝 *Note: This custom extraction approach was logged directly into the shop data matrix to bypass heavy special tool dependencies when field clearing frozen spark plug threads.*
